ABOUT COLLEGE

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is spread across five campuses in three states of India. Amrita School of Arts and Sciences functions under Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Kochi and located at Brahmasthanam Temple Complex at Edappally, Kochi, Kerala. The school offers various UG, PG and doctoral programs in various disciplines.
